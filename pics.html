Welcome to SOHO Parkway! This Metropolitan Hotel inspired condo features 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms and high-end finishes throughout.

Featuring a modern and stylish Gluckstein design highlighted by floor to ceiling windows and hardwood flooring throughout. Master bedroom offers double closet and 3 piece en-suite with glass walk-in shower. Full second 4 piece bathroom and convenient in-unit laundry. Generous 225 sq.ft balcony!

1 Underground parking and storage locker included. Building amenities include: private theatre room, soho sky club (roof top terrace with hot-tub), fitness center and meeting/conference room. Situated in sought after Tunny's Pasture location with its own light rail station coming soon! Only minutes to downtown and Parkdale Market.
